About

Fictitious is a community collaboration of creative writing projects.

Projects

Projects can be almost anything. You might be doing a one week project of Steampunk Cthulhu drabbles, a month-long 2,000 word rewriting of Moby Dick, or an ongoing wiki of alien pastry recipes. The number of people submitting to each Project will usually be limited to a manageable amount.
View all Projects

Pitches

Ideas for Projects are put forward as Pitches. The Pitch is the first step in decided a new Project and provides information about it, such as duration, word count, number of participants needed, and a description of the idea. If you’re interested in taking part in a Project leave a comment letting the Pitcher know.
View all Pitches.

If enough people are interested, a Pitch gets flagged as Active, with a To Do date.
View all Active Pitches

Taking Part

Please note that it’s very important that you’re happy to meet the Project requirements, such as deadlines and feedback. If you’re interested, but would prefer a slightly different word length, time limit, etc. leave a comment and discuss with the Pitcher.

Signing Up

You can take part in as many or as few Projects as you like, but we suggest you only do one at a time. You must be registered to comment or to post content, but you can use the same account for all the Projects you take part in.
